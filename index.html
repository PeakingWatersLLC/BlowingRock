The source

One property. One aquifer. One standard.

Our well sits on Edmisten Road in Blowing Rock, in the heart of the Blue Ridge. The water comes up cold, crisp, and naturally filtered through ancient mountain geology — subtle minerals, no off-flavors, tested to North Carolina well-water standards. It's the base of everything we bottle and everything we pour.
