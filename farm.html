Fraser Fir Farm & Christmas Stop

The tree stop on the way to the slopes.

Just down the road from Appalachian Ski Mtn, our Fraser fir farm is where High Country families make it a tradition — pick your tree, grab a wreath, warm up with a hot drink made from our well water, and bring the dog.

Getting here

On the road you're already driving.

Headed to Appalachian Ski Mtn? We're right down the road. Stop on the way up for a tree on the roof, or on the way down for a hot drink and a wreath. Season hours and directions will post here before opening weekend.
